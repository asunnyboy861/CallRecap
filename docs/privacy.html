Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 8, 2026

Introduction

CallRecap ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our iOS application.

Data We Collect

CallRecap is designed with privacy as a core principle. We collect minimal data:

  • Audio Recordings: Stored locally on your device. We never upload or transmit your recordings.
  • Transcripts & Summaries: Generated on-device using Whisper.cpp and Apple Intelligence. Stored locally with iCloud sync (encrypted).
  • Usage Data: We do not collect analytics, tracking data, or personal information.
  • Purchase Data: Subscription transactions are processed by Apple. We only receive confirmation of your subscription status.

On-Device Processing

All AI processing happens on your device:

  • Speech-to-text transcription uses Whisper.cpp running locally
  • AI summarization uses Apple Intelligence or local rule-based processing
  • No audio or text data is sent to external servers

iCloud Sync

If enabled, your recordings and transcripts sync via iCloud using CloudKit with end-to-end encryption. Apple's servers handle the transport, but your data is encrypted and inaccessible to us.

Permissions We Request

  • Microphone: Required to record calls and voice memos
  • Reminders: Optional, used to add action items from call summaries
  • Face ID / Touch ID: Optional, used for app lock feature

Data Retention

You have full control over your data. Recordings are stored until you delete them. Deleted recordings go to Trash for 30 days before permanent removal. You can permanently delete at any time.

Third-Party Services

CallRecap does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

Children's Privacy

CallRecap is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your data at any time through the app's settings. Since all data is stored on your device, you maintain complete control.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
