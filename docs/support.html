Managing Your Subscription

How to Cancel or Change Your Subscription

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. To cancel or change your subscription:

1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone
2. Tap your name at the top
3. Tap Subscriptions
4. Find CallRecap and tap it
5. Choose a different subscription option or tap Cancel Subscription

FAQ

Is my data private?

Yes. All audio transcription and AI summarization happens on your device. We never upload your recordings to any server.

Why does the app need microphone access?

CallRecap needs microphone access to record calls and voice memos. Without this permission, recording features will not work.

Does it work with all phone calls?

CallRecap detects incoming and outgoing calls via CallKit. Due to iOS restrictions, call audio is recorded from the microphone. For best results, use speakerphone during calls.

What's included in the free tier?

The free tier includes 5 transcriptions per month. Upgrading to Pro unlocks unlimited transcriptions, AI summaries, action items, export features, and Face ID lock.
